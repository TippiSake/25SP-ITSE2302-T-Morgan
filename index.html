Welcome to Houston Eastern European Resources Organization

Welcome to the Houston Eastern European Resource Organization, also known as H.E.R.O. We are here to support those who have come to Houston, Texas, especially Ukrainians and other refugees from Eastern Europe. Our mission is to connect people with the resources they need to not only survive but thrive. This includes providing essential items like food and housing, as well as opportunities to build relationships, join community groups, and create meaningful connections. At H.E.R.O., we honor the strength and bravery of refugees, who we see as heroes for their courage in difficult times. Through our website, we help these HEROes rebuild their lives with dignity by offering the tools and community support they need. We believe in the power of working together to create a caring space where everyone can find the help they need to live fully and confidently.

H.E.R.O. is dedicated to helping individuals and families find the specific resources they need for a successful transition. We assist in locating food banks and meal programs, affordable housing options, and employment opportunities tailored to their skills. We also connect refugees with education programs, including language courses and job training, to help them build a strong future. Understanding the importance of familiar communication, we offer guidance in finding doctors and healthcare providers who speak their language. We also connect individuals with local churches and civil organizations that share their cultural values and can offer additional support. At H.E.R.O., our goal is to ensure every person has access to the resources they need, not just to survive but to truly thrive in their new community.
